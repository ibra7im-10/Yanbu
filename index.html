Fully Soluble - SOP

Powder fertilizer, acidic and fully soluble in water, and rich in the sulfur content. It can be used in all kinds of irrigation systems to improve crop yield and quality.

Content Percentage (%)

SOP Potassium Sulphate

Product Description

Granular – SOP
0 / 0 / 50 + 17 S

Granular fertilizer, characterized by homogeneity of granules size, takes a long time to be completely dissolved in soil and it can be mixed with some granulated fertilizers during soil preparation (Spreading / ridging / dressing ..etc.).

Nutrient Composition:

Content Percentage (%)
Potassium (K2O)50
Potassium (K)41.6
Sulfur (S)17
Sulfate (SO4)42.5

Features:

  • White granular SOP, an ideal potash form for bulk blending with other straight fertilizers, to apply by spreading machines, during soil preparation (spreading / ridging / dressing... etc.).
  • It can apply to crops before planting or during winter service.
  • As a granulated fertilizer, it takes a long time to be completely dissolved in soil that makes Potassium more available for plants for a long time and increases the efficiency of applied fertilizer.
  • Rich in Sulfur, which increases the availability of Phosphorus and microelements in soil.

Packaging:

  • 25 kg
  • Jumpo bag

Applications:

  • To be used by spreading machines, during soil preparation (spreading/ridging/ dressing… etc.).
  • Can be used in fruit trees orchards before planting or during winter service.

Calcium Chloride 94% Granular

Calcium chloride is an inorganic compound, a salt with the chemical formula CaCl2. It is a white colored crystalline solid at room temperature, highly soluble in water.

CHARACTERISTICS 1_Calcium chloride lattices have a particularly large Hydration, so it is used in drought.
2_Calcium chloride solubility is very good in water: about 70g per 100 ml at 20°C.
3_Calcium chloride is also dissolved in ethanol: About 20g per 100 ml at 20°C.

USES 1. Due to the great being of calcium chloride for water, drying is applied as a major application.

2. CaCl2 is used as a food supplement in the canning and dairy industries to adjust acidity and promote milk coagulation during pasteurization.

3. Entered into other uses such as refineries, steel industries and fire extinguishing.
